Monopoly is a board game that has been played for over a century. It is a game of strategy and luck, where players buy and trade properties, build houses and hotels, and try to bankrupt their opponents. The game has been adapted into many different versions, featuring themes ranging from popular cities to beloved movies and TV shows.
